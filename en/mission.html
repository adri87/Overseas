    • Mission and corporate strategy

    MISSION

    The group's mission is to strengthen our business in the field of civil works internationally, with the approach of growth in an orderly and planned, and always under the standard of quality, efficiency and optimization. This value creation is always done in terms of economic, social and environmental.

    Given the experience and relationships of its Partners, OI&S wants to improve the quality of life of the citizens of the countries in which it operates. Therefore OI&S has chosen to undertake Engineering Projects, Construction and Services in the area of ​​civil works internationally.

    CORPORATE STRATEGY

    To achieve the objectives set by the Group, OI&S has a corporate strategy based on diversification of its clients and countries in which it operates, but always with the approach of sustainable growth, ordered and planned.

    Always following a strict long-term policy, OI&S follows strict selection criteria and recruitment and prudence in choosing their projects.

    On the economic situation in which it was founded OI&S, and understanding it as a profound change in the way of organizing and maintaining businesses, OI&S is committed to a strong commitment to maintaining an economic structure solid, raising much of their economic resources funded own partners and investors and long-term financial resources.
