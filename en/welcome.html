    • Welcome letter

    On behalf of OI&S, I extend a warm welcome to our website that we hope will help to expand your knowledge about our group.

    OI&S is a company born in 2009 with the aim of becoming a leader in the field of Spanish civil works internationally through its three lines of business, engineering, construction and services. Diversifying Countries we develop projects, excellent international relations and other companies and commitment to quality and excellence are the main stakes of the Group.

    Sustainable development is one of the values ​​that the Group is committed to encouraging, always thinking of future generations. Therefore all our actions are governed by policies that ensure that the environment does not suffer any prejudice by them. In this sense, in OI&S count with ISO 14001 certification whose main lines are the identification and evaluation for each work of significant environmental aspects and establishing practices that minimize the impact and control of the operations with the order to verify its effectiveness and suitability of the Environmental Management System.
    The quality and excellence in our work as references. These values ​​represent the character with which we try to develop all our projects. So in O.I.&S. We have ISO 9001 certification, accreditation gives us the guarantee of quality in all processes in which we intervene.

    OI&S feels a special commitment to all its employees, its subcontractors and all staff employed in different human activities. This commitment is reflected in the OHSAS 18001 certification, an internationally recognized assessment specification for environmental management systems of health and safety at work, which accreditation OI&S is proud to tell.

    Through this medium we make available a wealth of information about our group, its goals, commitments and other information that may be relevant. Finally I thank you all and wish to have your confidence to progress further, so I invite you to delve into the content of these pages and their awareness of who we are and what we do.



    Welcome to our website
    Mariano Rodriguez Llama
    President of OI&S
