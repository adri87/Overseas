    • History

    The activity of OI & S was founded in September 2009 with the task of drafting a major works related to the water cycle, which includes, among others, the activities of collection, treatment, transmission and supply of water in the Republic of Equatorial Guinea for a significant number of municipalities with significant horizon population growth.

    After the project of the Republic of Equatorial Guinea, and lessons, international relations and trade relations with other Spanish and foreign companies in the sector, OI & S expands its customer base and diversified operations in other countries, currently developing projects more than 10 countries on three continents.

    OI&S still being a young group, has the extensive experience of its members, who are developing the activity in the sector for over 25 years.
