    OI&S is an international engineering, construction and services. Its activity focuses exclusively on the international market, developing civil works projects, both in planning and execution and services, in South America, Africa and Europe.

    Drawing on the extensive experience of the partners in the area of ​​design and construction in buildings, and with excellent international relations at the institutional level, OI & S. was founded for international civil engineering projects for government agencies, but also undertakes projects for private entities.

    OI&S is working to expand its field of activity, its resources and its business volume, keeping in mind the commitment to offer the maximum guarantee of quality, innovation and improvement in all projects in which it participates. The diversification of its customers and the countries in which develops projects, is one of the guarantees for which the Group bet, given the global economic situation.
