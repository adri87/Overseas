    • Value creation

    OI&S has acquired own responsibility corporate value creation, considering it in all disciplines, and developing it throughout your business environment that includes, among others, customers, employees, suppliers, partners ...

    Customers

    The customer, our reason for being. In order to satisfy all the demands and requirements of our customers, OI&S using the latest technologies in their production and construction techniques, always offering high quality solutions to meet the expectations of our customers.

    Employees

    The employees are the driving force behind OI&S to continuous development and growth. For this reason the Group pays special attention to promote their teams, through continuous training, development of dynamic work environments and challenging, offering good working conditions and family ...
    Moreover, OI&S pays special care when outsourced staff work areas. The Group follows strict criteria when making personnel outsourcing, demanding quality standards, respect for the environment and safety at work that OI&S continues to be fulfilled.

    Suppliers

    To create value their jobs, OI&S has the help of its suppliers. For this reason, and to continue with the standards followed by OI&S, its suppliers require the same quality as the group offers its customers.
    This will get full circle of value creation.
